L&F Holding Has been formed to create a multisectoral group opening in various activities such as industry, the aerospace industry, the construction ,Public works, Renewable energy, the environment, new technologies and associated services.

Diversified portfolio

The group account today more than 12 companies and owns sites in France, Italy, Morocco, Senegal, Turkey, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Saudi Arabia and recently, India and China. The development and strategy of the group will be focused on activities with high added value and on regions with high growth potential.

Strong model of Governance

L&F Holding is Fort of a culture both in financial and industrial, the Group articulated its mission around the following ambitions :

Invest in innovative sectors, To strong growth and wealth creators for the various companies of the group.

Initiate strategic partnerships With private operators or national and international public through joint investment plans in different areas of activity.

Continue the development of existing trades With strong potential and rebalance the portfolio of areas of activity.

Ensure a position of leader In each of the sectors of activity invested.

Experienced team

The team of L&F Holding Is one of the most experienced of the place, consisting of engineers, economists, lawyers, architects, experts in finance ...
The sectoral organization with specialized professionals in each of the areas of specialization.
These sectoral teams detect the best investment opportunities and work in close collaboration with the leaders in the development andthe realization of the business project.

Culture of enterprise developer

L&F Holding Is the first priority is to the growth of businesses of the group in a long-term perspective, playing on all the levers of value creation: organic growth, external growth, improvement of the economic model to make it more sustainable, development at the international, etc...
